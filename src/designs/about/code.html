Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data actually stored?

Your data is shredded and encrypted into fragments stored across decentralized IPFS nodes. Only your private wallet key can reassemble and decrypt it.

Can insurance companies see my data?

No. By default, your identity is anonymous. You choose exactly who sees what via granular on-chain permissions.

What can I do with Healia tokens?

Tokens can be used to unlock premium AI insights, purchase biological age tests, or staked to participate in protocol governance.

Is Healia a medical device?

Healia is a health optimization protocol. While we use peer-reviewed science, we recommend consulting a doctor for any clinical decisions.
